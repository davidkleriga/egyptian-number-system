Egyptian Unit Fractions

A fraction written as a sum of distinct unit fractions

So What is This About?


The ancient Egyptians were possibly the first civilisation to practice the scientific arts. Indeed, the word chemistry is derived from the word Alchemy which is the ancient name for Egypt. The Egyptians really excelled in medicine and applied mathematics. But although there is a large body of papyrus literature describing their achievements in medicine, there are no records of how they reached their mathematical conclusions. They must have had an advanced understanding of the subject,because their exploits in engineering, astronomy and administration would not have been possible without it.

Requirements

You are to create a simple web page that takes as input two positive integers: a numerator and a denominator, representing a positive rational fraction.

After entering the two numbers, the page should display the representation of that fraction as an egyptian fraction

Your program should always use the largest unit possible[1], and display the components in descending order.

Test Cases
